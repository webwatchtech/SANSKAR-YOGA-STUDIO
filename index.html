About Myself

Since childhood, I was immersed in the teachings of Sanatan Vedic Dharma, inspired by my grandfather and father who read the Bhagavad Gita daily. My fascination with yoga began in the fifth grade and deepened as I explored Shatkarma kriyas by the seventh grade. Reading Patanjali Yog Darshan ignited a lifelong passion for yoga.

Over the past 3 to 4 years, I have been conducting yoga sessions both online and offline, adapting to changing circumstances while staying committed to my practice.

In 2020, I founded Sanskar Yoga Studio. Despite the challenges of the lockdown, I have resumed operations, offering a variety of yoga classes including home sessions. My expertise includes Patanjali Yoga Darshan, Ashtanga Yoga, yoga nidra, hatha yoga, shatkarma, pranayama, and meditation.

Recently, under the guidance of Prashant Acharya ji at Vedic Gurukul Alwar, I have furthered my studies in Nyaya Darshan, Bhagavad Gita, and Yoga Darshan. I remain dedicated to exploring and teaching the profound philosophies of yoga.

Join me on a journey to inner peace and holistic wellness through the transformative power of yoga.
